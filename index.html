Year in review 2019-2020

Welcome to our first annual Year in Review, a new approach and format which allows us to share some of our key achievements over the past year with you, our stakeholders.

Using the SAD reporting document we have extracted and illustrated some metrics to show progress we have made in key areas.

We also take a deeper dive into some important areas with case studies.

This is the first year of our new NES Strategy 2019-2024, A Skilled and Sustainable Workforce for a Healthier Scotland.

Some key aims and objectives of the strategy.

Our Year in Review features:

  • Introduction and context
  • 6 case studies
  • Infographics based on SAD document

We hope you enjoy this snapshot of our work over the past year.
